About Net Foundation

Net Foundation (NF-NPO) is a registered non-profit organization committed to empowering vulnerable and underserved young people. We believe in creating opportunities for education, personal growth, and sustainable futures.

Established with a vision to make a meaningful difference in our communities, we work tirelessly to provide support, resources, and guidance to those who need it most.

Our organization operates as a body corporate, existing separately from its members, with the ability to own property and operate in its own capacity to best serve our mission.

Our Mission & Objectives

Practical Support

Collect and distribute sanitary towels, toiletries, clothes, and food parcels for families in need.

Health & Wellbeing

Encourage young people to take responsibility for their own health and wellbeing through education and support.

Education Support

Motivate and encourage the importance of education by offering academic support to our communities.

Career Guidance

Provide career guidance to learners, helping them realize their potential and build sustainable futures.
